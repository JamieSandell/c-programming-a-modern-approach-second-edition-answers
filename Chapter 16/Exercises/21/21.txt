What are the integer values of the enumeration constants in each of the following declara-
tions?

(a) enum {NUL, SOH, STX, ETX};
(b) enum {VT = 11, FF, CR};
(c) enum {SO = 14, ST, DLE, CAN = 24, EM};
(d) enum {ENQ = 45, ACK, BEL, LF = 37, ETB, ESC};

(a) 0, 1, 2, 3.
(b) 11, 12, 13.
(c) 14, 15, 16, 24, 25.
(d) 45, 46, 47, 37, 38, 39.
